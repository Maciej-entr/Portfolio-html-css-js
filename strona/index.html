About Me

Hello! I'm Maciej, a passionate front-end developer with a knack for creating visually appealing and user-friendly web experiences. With no work-like experience in the field, I specialize in transforming design concepts into responsive and interactive websites that delight users. I have a strong foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with expertise in modern frameworks like React and Vue.js. I thrive on collaboration, often working closely with designers and back-end developers to ensure that projects meet both aesthetic and functional requirements. Beyond coding, I have a keen interest in user experience (UX) and accessibility, believing that great design should be inclusive and accessible to everyone. I constantly stay updated with the latest trends and technologies in web development, which helps me enhance my skills and deliver high-quality results. In my spare time, you can find me experimenting with new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing my knowledge through blogging and mentoring. I’m always eager to learn and grow, and I'm excited to take on new challenges in the ever-evolving world of web development. Let's connect and create something amazing together!
